// About Us //

Avanthi Motors Is The Best Place For Your Auto Care

Avanthi sets itself apart in the realm of restoration and auto refurbishment, offering a comprehensive range of unparalleled services. Renowned for its unwavering commitment to quality and reliability, Avanthi is synonymous with excellence in the eyes of its loyal clientele. The tradition at Avanthi revolves around meticulous care and upholding its reputation for total reliability in handling customers' assets, ensuring they are returned in flawless condition. The intangible asset of customer trust holds the utmost value at Avanthi, with a steadfast dedication to meeting and exceeding high expectations. Customer satisfaction is not just a goal but a mission, driving Avanthi to never falter in delivering on promises, regardless of the cost. Maintaining its position at the top is imperative, as anything less would leave customers feeling let down. The continual growth and enhancement of facilities are solely dedicated to serving the needs of the customers, ensuring Avanthi remains at the pinnacle of its industry.

From Kerala to Mumbai

Davis Anthony, the driving force behind this venture, embarked on his journey at the tender age of 14 when he left his native Kerala in India and arrived in Mumbai back in 1974. Starting from humble beginnings, he immersed himself in the world of auto refurbishment and restoration under the tutelage of seasoned masters of the trade. Through unwavering dedication and a relentless pursuit of excellence, he ascended to the esteemed position of Foreman within the same organization. Renowned for his innate ability to observe intricacies and a steadfast commitment to enhancing quality, Davis earned a sterling reputation for delivering impeccable refurbishment projects. Since his early days in Mumbai, he has been in high demand as a skilled technician for restoring classic and vintage cars.

Beginning of own workshop

Davis Anthony prioritized acquiring state-of-the-art equipment crucial to his trade, recognizing its significance. However, he understood that skilled hands were equally indispensable. According to Davis, the right attitude and a relentless commitment to quality from his assistants were key in upholding his reputation. Assisting him in his relentless pursuit of excellence are his partners turned Directors, Sattendra Rawat, Mahendra Rawat, and his brother William Donald. Each partner is an expert in their respective fields, collectively possessing an encyclopedic knowledge of classic and vintage cars. Renowned as technocrats in the auto refurbishment sphere, they are highly esteemed by those closely associated with the industry.

Facilities at Pune (two places) and at Kolhapur.

Avanthi Motor Works Pvt. Ltd. has set up cutting-edge facilities in Pune and Kolhapur, equipped with advanced technology and resources for restoration and refurbishment projects. The company's expansion into multiple locations, including Pune and Kolhapur, reflects their commitment to growth and serving a broader customer base. These strategic expansions have enabled Avanthi Motor Works to enhance its reach and capabilities in catering to the needs of classic and vintage car enthusiasts.

At Present

Since Davis Anthony commenced operations in 1990, he has meticulously groomed his team to embody the organization's philosophy. With minimal labor turnover, his workforce remains highly skilled and dedicated, consistently advancing the company's values. Remarkably, turnover among highly skilled denting and painting specialists has been virtually nonexistent over the past 22 years, reflecting the organization's commitment to fair remuneration and rewards. Currently, there are over 21 skilled and highly trained individuals, supported by 48 additional workers in various roles, including those being groomed for higher positions. The company boasts extensive contacts and capabilities to procure rare parts and equipment, ensuring optimal resources for every project. Furthermore, they maintain a vigilant eye on industry advancements, often pioneering the adoption of cutting-edge techniques. With a workshop area spanning approximately 40,000 square feet in Pune alone (with other units similarly equipped), the company is well-equipped to handle projects of any scale. Their expertise has been consistently recognized through awards and accolades garnered by customers at vintage and classic car rallies across various cities in India.
